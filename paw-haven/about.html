Our Story

A Home for Every Wagging Tail

Paw Haven began in 2016 when a small group of volunteers noticed how many healthy pets were overlooked simply because families didn't know where to look. What started as a single foster home in Abuja has grown into a network connecting verified shelters across Nigeria with loving adopters.

Today, we've helped over 250 pets find homes — and we're just getting started.

🎯 Our Mission

To rehome healthy, well-cared-for pets with families who are ready to give them a lifetime of love, while supporting shelters with resources and adopters with lifelong guidance.

🌅 Our Vision

A future where no healthy, adoptable pet is left waiting — and every shelter has the support it needs to care for the animals in its charge.

Our Journey

Milestones Along the Way

2016 — The First Foster Home

Paw Haven opens its doors in Abuja with a single foster home and five rescued dogs.

2018 — Expanding the Network

We partner with our first 10 verified shelters across Lagos and Port Harcourt.

2021 — 100th Adoption

We celebrate our 100th successful adoption and launch our lifetime support hotline.

2026 — 250+ Happy Homes

Paw Haven now supports 40 shelters nationwide and continues to grow every month.
